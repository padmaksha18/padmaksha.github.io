    About me

    As a PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at Virginia Tech, I am privileged to work under the guidance of Dr. Ming Jin and being part of Hume Center & CCI. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning techniques in anomaly detection, robustness in the presence of adversaries, and out-of-distribution generalization. Applications include cybersecurity, cyber-physical intrusion detection, and time series anomaly detection. Currently, I am exploring ways to leverage Transformer models (GPT, BERT etc) for OOD attack detection in various kinds of cyber-security datasets.
